Welcome!

I'm a graduate student at OHSU, in the Baconguis lab. We work on structural biochemistry, specifically cryoEM of ion channels. Check out our recent paper on the structure of ENaC!

If you do any chromatography, you might be interested in my HPLC analysis scripts. They're written for a Waters HPLC, because that's what we have, but if you submit an issue along with an example output file of your HPLC I'd be happy to take a look.

I've got a science twitter, and I'm also involved in our new grad student union. I was one of the first organizers, and it's been so inspiring (and challenging) helping grow it from a few of us whining at a bar to something that takes everyones thoughts into account and works to make OHSU a better place to study. If you have any questions about forming a union at your university (or any other place of work) please feel free to send me an email!
