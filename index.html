Welcome!

This is what I look like when I'm drinking a beer on the alvord desert!

I'm a graduate student at OHSU, in the Baconguis lab. We work on structural biochemistry, specifically cryoEM of ion channels. Check out our first and second papers on the structure and function of ENaC!

In addition to protein biochemistry, I enjoy writing scripts to help make repeat analysis easier. Probably my biggest accomplishments are my chromatography analysis scripts, which incorporate collating and plotting data from Waters and AKTA instruments with a web interface that you can access from anywhere. I got tired of copying files and making .csvs manually! Take a look here! I also enjoy reading, cycling, backpacking, and cooking. My goal this year is to cook my way through a new cookbook!
